Hiii, this is maffinnn

"Code shapes the world; art teaches me how to see it."

I'm a Full-Stack Software Engineer who moves between two modes of creation. In one, I'm building systems that hold their shape under pressure; in the other, I'm following lines and shadows to understand how ideas take form.

My work has drawn me toward problems that require patience and precision. I now build core product at a tech company, shaped by time in fast-moving teams where I learned what it means to design systems that stay efficient even as they grow.

Beyond the screen, drawing is where I return to quiet. Whether with a pencil or a stylus, it's the place where I slow down, pay attention, and let the world reveal itself a little at a time.
